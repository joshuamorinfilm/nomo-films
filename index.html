About

NOMO Films has over a decade of experience filming travel videos in over 40 countries. We have won two Vimeo Staff Pick awards and have filmed for over 30 tourism boards worldwide. Our diverse portfolio ranges from viral travel videos to award winning documentaries to high-end product commercials. We are passionate about telling meaningful stories and helping others fulfill their artistic visions.

NOMO Films is based in NYC and Toronto
