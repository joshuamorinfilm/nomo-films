Project: 30 second TVC Desert Dental
Client: Desert Dental
Producer: Ansley Sawyer
Director: Joshua Morin
DP: Blaze Nowara

How can we make a commercial for a permanent denture company interesting?

This is the question we were asking ourselves when this client approached us. The client wanted it to be emotional, creative, and target ages 65+. We decided to focus on the relationship between a grandfather and his granddaughter and the barrier between them caused by uncomfortable and unflattering dentures.
