Wherever you are in the world, I hope you are doing well. It's hard to put this video into words. It's about people. People from all over the world, wherever they may be. It is a video we just felt like creating for the sake of creation. We used portraits of friends, family and people we have met over the years to question the moment and perhaps draw strength from. This video has been in the making over the past 4 months. We were right at the end of our edit when our internal hard drive crashed and set us back quite a bit. It's hard to pick up from the near beginning and re-edit what you have already done in the past. We only mention this because we are usually very careful about backups, but since this one wasn't for a client it slipped through our traditional backup routine. Daily backups, regardless of the project are now our normal :)
