WHO IS NOMO

A BIT ABOUT US

NOMO creates and shapes culture through cinematic pieces that span the worlds of commercials, documentaries, music videos, and films. We’re an emotions-first studio that defiantly pursues work the world wants to talk about.

Capturing visually stunning shots that evoke emotion is our specialty. With headquarters in New York and Toronto, we’ve produced projects in over 50 countries, refining a roster of diverse creators across the globe.

 

At NOMO, we uphold the best and most visually refined stories that resonate deeply and evoke actual feelings.

THE STORIES

In order to cast as wide a net as possible it’s crucial to diversify stories, settings and cast. In order to do that, we’ll create three short videos for each of the three Core Challenges. That’s 9 videos total.
