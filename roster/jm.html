Director - Joshua Morin

Director, filmmaker, Joshua Morin began his career making moving images in 2010, specializing in travel filmmaking, capturing moments that were never to happen again for social brands and social media. It was years later when commissioned on a project for Sean Penn's NGO in Haiti that Joshua decided to focus on projects, subjects and ideas that create change in the world.
Since then, he has filmed for wide range of NGOs, commercial brands and television shows all around the world that are doing good in the world. His most recent short film called Osita depicts the struggle of a displaced family, like so many in this world, who struggles to find a place in the world that provides healthcare and safety for their children.
Joshua has recently filmed four television shows for AlJazzera to showcase travel in Qatar for the World cup as well as another television show for the Discovery Channel to feature Poznan in Poland.
