About

"Ever since I picked up a camera, I have been searching for moments that arrive never to be seen again. I strive to capture these moments whether in a commercial environment or in remote area of the world."

Joshua has filmed for National Geographic, Chaco Footwear and many others. He has made films for many tourism boards all over the world such as Jordan, Austria, Canada, Italy.
