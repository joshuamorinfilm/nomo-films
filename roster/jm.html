Director - Joshua Morin
Director, filmmaker, Joshua Morin began his career making moving images in 2010, specializing in travel filmmaking, capturing moments that were never to happen again for social brands. It was years later when commissioned on a project for Sean Penn's NGO in Haiti that Joshua decided to focus on projects, subjects and ideas that create change in the world.

Since then, he has filmed for wide range of NGOs, commercial brands and television shows all around the world that are making an impact for better. His most recent short film called Osita depicts a displaced mother and child who struggle to find a place in the world that provide proper healthcare and safety for her child.

Joshua has recently filmed four television shows for AlJazzera to showcase travel in Qatar for World cup as well as a Discovery Channel show featuring adventure travel in Poznan in Poland.

Joshua loves ultimate frisbee and working on his tiny home which his family live in when not abroad.
