In 2019, it was reported that an estimated 970 million people around the globe were living with a mental disorder. The most common disorders people deal with are anxiety and depression, which during the Covid-19 pandemic, increased across the global population by 25%. Maybelline New York launched Brave Together in 2020 as a response to this crisis. A programme committed to opening up the conversation about mental health, itâ€™s goal is to make support for anxiety and depression accessible to everyone, everywhere. Through the programme, Maybelline New York provides funding to local mental health non-governmental organisations worldwide. It supports them in delivering free, essential mental health services including one-to-one professional support via helplines and in-person student trainings.
